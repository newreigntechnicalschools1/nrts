Our Curriculum

We follow a Nigerian based curriculum with elements of the British curriculum. Our learning hours is from 8am to 4pm.
We do all necessary and basic subject for all classes from Kindergarten to SS3 including elective subjects in higher levels or classes.

Other activities

We offer school lessons that continues after school hours. If interested you can discuss it with the secretary at the school building or through our social media platforms on our contacts page
We also partake in complex sporting activities to keep the students physically and mentally intact.

School Policies


Integrity Policy: Our school is commited to upholding the highest standards of academic and personal integrity. All students are expected to complete their own work and avoid plagiarism or cheating in any form. Any violation of this policy will result in appropriate dsiciplinary action.

Attendance Policy: Regular attendance is expected of all students. Any absences or tardiness must be approved in advance by a parent or guardian. Excessive absences or tardiness may result in disciplinary action and may negatively impact a student's grade.


Some other policies are listed below:


Code of Conduct: Proper behaviour is expected from all students. Disrespect to fellow students or staff will not be tolerated.
Dress Code: The proper uniforms for each day must be followed by all students to avoid issues with the school management.
Bullying Prevention: No act of bullying will be tolerated and the offender will be strictly disciplined, that being said if any students faces any act of bullying it should be reported immediately to any staff or the proprietress.
Technology Use: The use of electronic devices are prohibited unless a teacher demands the need for it in a classroom.
Extracurricular Activities: Students should endeavour to participate in all school activities that are mandatory.
