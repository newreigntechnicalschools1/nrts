About Us

New Reign Technical Schools is a leading private institution committed to academic excellence and moral integrity.
We offer a dynamic learning environment with qualified teachers and modern facilities. Our students consistently perform well in both national and international examinations. We promote leadership, innovation, and character development in all learners. Our main goal is to create great leaders of tomorrow.

Our Values and Vision

Core Values:

1. Academic Excellence: The school is committed to providing a rigorous and challenging academic curriculum that prepares students for success in university and beyond.
2. Respect and inclusivity: The school values diversity and promotes a culture of respect, inclusivity, and mutual understanding among all members of the school community.
3. Character Development: The school emphasizes the importance of developing students' character, including their values, ethics, and social responsibility.
4. Collaboration and Teamwork: The school values collaborationa nd teamwork, and promotes opportunities for students to work together to acheive common goals.
5. Creation and Innovation: The school encourages creativity and innovation in all areas of study and promotes a culture of experimentation, risk-taking, and problem-solving.

Vision Statement:

"To provide a transformative education that inspires every child to reach their life, and become full potentials, excel in all areas of life, and become future leaders with integrity and purpose."
